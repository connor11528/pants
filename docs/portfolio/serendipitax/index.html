Serendipitax

Client: 17 Productions

Category: Key Art

Project:
Art direction and design for an independent short film.

Execution:
I manipulated the photographs and constructed a movie poster, key art, for promotion. The director wanted a Girl with the Dragon Tattoo feel, so I used the re-release materials as inspiration for this design.

Collaboration with the same crew of Estranged, I was once again commissioned to create key art for the group’s, named 17 Productions, next indie short film. Serendipitax is about Michael’s chance to recruit an inmate to his side, but his own psychological paranoia taxed his potential to save her.

Photography by the talented Krystal Hills.
